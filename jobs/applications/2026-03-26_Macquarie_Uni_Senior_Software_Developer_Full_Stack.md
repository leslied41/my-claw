## Job Description
Macquarie University is seeking an innovative and forward-thinking Senior Software Developer (Full Stack) for a role dedicated to building and scaling AI enablement capability. You will develop, test, deploy and maintain Generative AI enabled solutions. You will provide technical support and advice to users with Generative AI enabled solutions. You will be part of a newly created AI Enablement team within IT and will play a crucial role in integrating AI solutions across various faculties and departments.

Key responsibilities:
- Co-ordinate and execute the design, development, implementation, operation and user training for Generative AI solutions
- Develop Proof of Concept Gen-AI solutions in Azure or AWS
- Set up, run and monitor automated test plans
- Deploy approved solutions in production environment
- Develop intuitive front-end interfaces using modern frontend frameworks (e.g. React)
- Create & maintain comprehensive documentation for AI models, APIs, and deployment processes

Required: Proficiency in Python, JavaScript, familiarity with R. Experience with NLP techniques and libraries for text generation and understanding. Proficiency in cloud platforms (AWS and Azure). Strong understanding of CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes. Experience developing React front-ends.
