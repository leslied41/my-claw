## Job Description
Medbill is on a journey to better support specialist doctors in providing healthcare across Australia. Over the past 15 years, we have become a leader in supporting doctors to administer high-quality care for their patients. We strive to support our doctors with their administrative tasks so they are able to spend more time with patients driving positive health outcomes.

The business is currently undergoing a period of rapid growth and requires a full-time Software Engineer to join the team. This is a hybrid role based in Brisbane; working from home is supported, but spending regular time in the office with the engineering team and operational users is considered key to successful collaboration.

The Opportunity:
- Take responsibility for the hands-on design, development, and delivery of software solutions across the company's technology function.
- Contribute to the continuous improvement of our core platforms, including our internal web application and practitioner Progressive Web Application (PWA).
- Write clean, secure, scalable, and maintainable code to shift the Medbill application ecosystem toward a SaaS model.
- Participate actively in the full software development life cycle, from requirements gathering to deployment, automated testing, and support.
- Review existing cloud-based systems and implement improvements to support scalability, security, and maintainability.
- Identify opportunities to automate manual workflows and improve operational efficiency.
- Utilise AI agentic toolsets (such as Claude Code, OpenCode, Gemini CLI, or Codex) to enhance productivity and accelerate delivery.
- Work collaboratively with operational teams and communicate clearly regarding task progress and technical blockers.

Your Skills and Experience:
- Proven commercial experience (typically 3+ years) in software engineering roles.
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript and Node.js for back-end development.
- Hands-on experience building and deploying on AWS, including familiarity with ECS, Lambda, Aurora, and AWS CDK.
- Experience contributing to modern web and mobile applications, with familiarity in React or React-based frameworks being beneficial.
- Solid understanding of relational databases like PostgreSQL and building or consuming RESTful APIs.
- Experience working with CI/CD pipelines, automated testing such as Jest, and Git-based version control.
- Strong communication skills with the ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
- Ability to operate with a healthy balance of autonomy and teamwork.
- Desirable: Previous experience or familiarity with the health care, medical billing, or insurance domains.
