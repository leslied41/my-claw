## Job Description
Location: Sydney
 At Prospa, we exist to unleash the potential of small business. We are building the future of small business and simplifying cashflow by building a leading platform that helps customers make payments, fund growth and reduce admin. Join us!
The role
As a Senior Full Stack Engineer, you'll help build the technology that enables small businesses to move forward with confidence. You'll work across the stack to design, build and scale intuitive, secure and reliable systems that make it easier for our customers to access the tools and funding they need to succeed.
You'll be part of a modern, cloud‑native, microservices environment, partnering closely with Product, Design and Engineering to turn ideas into high‑quality customer experiences. You'll take ownership of your work end‑to‑end, think customer‑first, and contribute to a team that values learning, experimentation and continuous improvement.
We actively use AI‑powered developer tooling to improve code quality, speed up delivery, and reduce cognitive load, freeing you up to focus on solving meaningful problems
What you'll be doing

Designing, building and shipping high‑quality product features across front‑end and back‑end systems
Owning technical problems from discovery through to production, with a focus on long‑term, scalable solutions
Using AI‑assisted development tools (e.g. code generation, refactoring, test support) to accelerate delivery while maintaining strong engineering standards
Collaborating with product managers and designers to deliver simple, fast and reliable customer experiences
Ensuring systems are observable, well‑monitored, secure and resilient in production
Keeping security, privacy and compliance front of mind throughout the SDLC
Contributing to architecture discussions, code reviews and continuous improvement initiatives
Sharing knowledge, experimenting with new tools and techniques, and helping lift the team around you

What you'll need to succeed

Strong hands‑on experience with .NET Framework/Core and React with TypeScript (hooks, component‑based architecture)
Experience integrating front‑end applications with internal and third‑party APIs
Solid understanding of modern software architecture patterns, including microservices and event‑driven systems
Experience working in cloud‑based environments (Azure experience is a plus)Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, automated testing and modern DevOps practices
Proficiency with Git and modern build tools (e.g. npm, Webpack)
Working knowledge of web accessibility standardsA passion for clean, secure, well‑tested code and building software that makes a real customer impact
Curiosity and openness to using AI tools to improve how you build, test and maintain software
