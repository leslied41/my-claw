Are you a Senior Developer who enjoys building high-quality products in a fast-moving, low-bureaucracy environment? We're partnering with a growing Australian fintech/wealth-tech business that is scaling rapidly, moving from one client to multiple enterprise customers in a short period.

The Opportunity:
You'll play a key role in designing, building, and evolving a modern platform used across financial services clients. Working closely with senior stakeholders and a tight-knit development team, you'll contribute across the full stack while helping shape engineering practices as the business scales.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and enhance full stack applications using Java (Spring Boot) and React
- Build and integrate APIs across internal and external systems
- Contribute to scalable, cloud-native solutions on AWS
- Support and evolve reporting capabilities (including PDF-based outputs)
- Collaborate closely with business stakeholders to deliver intuitive, high-quality solutions
- Participate in Agile delivery (stand-ups, planning, retrospectives)
- Contribute to continuous improvement of development practices and tooling

Skills & Experience:
- Proven experience as a Senior Full Stack Developer
- Strong backend development in Java / Spring Boot
- Frontend expertise in React
- Solid experience with SQL / PostgreSQL
- Exposure to AWS (e.g. Lambda, cloud-native services) is advantageous
- Experience building APIs and working with distributed systems
- Background in financial services or regulated environments (highly regarded)
- Experience with reporting tools / PDF generation (nice to have)
- Strong communication skills

Bonus / Future-Focused Skills:
- Experience leveraging AI tools in software development workflows

Why Join:
- Be part of a rapidly scaling business with strong client demand
- Work on meaningful, real-world financial platforms
- Influence architecture, tooling, and ways of working
- High-autonomy environment with minimal red tape
- Competitive salary with long-term growth opportunity
