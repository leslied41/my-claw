## Job Description
About Synechron:
Synechron is a global technology consulting firm that helps leading organizations accelerate digital transformation through innovation, expertise and agility. With more than 16,500 professionals across around 60 offices in 20 countries, we combine deep industry knowledge with advanced capabilities in such areas as AI, cloud, cybersecurity, and data engineering.
Our regional teams, supported by strategic delivery centers, provide scalable, cost-efficient solutions tailored to local markets. Through our award-winning Synechron FinLabs accelerators and strategic partnerships with AWS, Microsoft, Databricks, Salesforce, and ServiceNow, we enable clients to innovate fast and lead with confidence. For more information on the company, please visit our website or LinkedIn community.
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Software Engineer who prides themselves on designing, developing, and maintaining enterprise-grade software applications. This role requires strong collaboration with cross-functional teams, a passion for building secure and scalable systems, and the ability to integrate modern technologies including AI/ML.
Key Responsibilities

Design, develop, and maintain enterprise software applications.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
Work closely with DevSecOps teams to ensure secure and automated deployments.
Partner with product management to align technical solutions with business objectives.
Troubleshoot, debug, and upgrade existing systems for optimal performance and reliability.
Implement robust security measures, data protection strategies, and fail-safe redundancy features.

Required Skills & Experience

Strong proficiency in Node.js, TypeScript, and Express (Fastify experience is also welcome).
Experience with NestJS (good to have).
Hands-on experience with AWS services (e.g., S3, ECS, Lambda).
Familiarity with observability tools such as CloudWatch, Prometheus, Grafana.
Experience with CI/CD tools: GitHub, GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, and automated deployment pipelines.
Exposure to AI/ML technologies with the ability to integrate or leverage AI models for enhanced automation and intelligence.
Solid experience with messaging technologies (MQ, Kafka).
Strong knowledge of SQL and NoSQL databases.
Proven experience applying DevSecOps methodologies and best practices to automate deployments and testing.

What We're Looking For

A proactive problem solver with strong debugging and troubleshooting skills.
Someone who thrives in collaborative environments and can work closely with product and DevSecOps teams.
An engineer passionate about building secure, scalable, and intelligent systems.
