A high-growth fintech is building systems that directly impact how everyday Australians access and manage their finances - from lending and payments to real-time decisioning.This is a hands-on senior engineering role in a small, high-performing squad where your work will shape real systems in production, not sit behind layers of process.The RoleYou'll join a cross-functional team working on a core lending platform, contributing across design, build, and run.This is not a ticket-driven environment.
