## Job Description
Optus is an Australian telecommunications company, delivering more than 11 million services to our customers every day across mobile, broadband and digital solutions.The Software Engineer designs, develops, and maintains automation software that supports the Networks Division, working within a cross‑functional engineering team to build reliable and secure applications. They collaborate closely with the Lead Software Developer to deliver features, align with technical direction, and integrate with broader automation frameworks. The role spans the full software lifecycle—development, testing, deployment, documentation, and support—while driving continuous improvement and reusable tooling. Your day to day will consist of Deliver and maintain high‑quality software products as part of the Networks Automation and AI Development team. Contribute to code reviews to support quality, maintainability, and adherence to internal engineering standards. Develop and implement components based on low‑level design specifications, ensuring accuracy, performance, and architectural alignment. Produce clear and complete technical documentation to support operational teams, engineering workflows, and continuous maintenance. Provide technical support for complex issues, collaborating with the Lead Software Developer to apply best practice engineering approaches Participate in Tier 3 support activities, including incident investigation, software upgrades, patching, and lifecycle maintenance. Support monitoring of product KPIs and assist in ensuring compliance with cybersecurity requirements and internal governance practices. Collaborate with engineering teams to enable adoption of product features, reusable tools, and automation capabilities. Maintain and update platform documentation, configuration baselines, and operational runbooks.  What makes you perfect for the role 4+ years in Software Development, DevOps, or Automation roles. Solid understanding of software security and compliance fundamentals.  Experience working with databases such as PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Redis Cache. Experience with programming languages including Python and JavaScript/TypeScript (Ansible scripting desirable). Familiarity with tools such as GitLab, Ansible Automation Platform, MinIO, Apache Airflow, and Mapbox. Familiarity with observability tooling (e.g., ELK, Prometheus, Grafana). Hands-on experience with CI/CD tools (e.g., GitLab CI, Jenkins, Azure DevOps). Familiarity with technologies & platforms including React, Node.js, FastAPI, Red Hat OpenShift, and GIS. Experience working in agile, cross‑functional environments. Previous experience with telecommunications networks or network automation is desirable. AI Agent development experience is highly desirable.  What's in it for you? Competitive remuneration and employee discounts. Make life easier (and more affordable) with $80 monthly credit and 25% off Optus products and unique shopping discounts with our retail partners. Competitive leave policies, including additional 'Connected' days to focus on culture, family, health, community, or whatever's important to you. We support growing families with inclusive, carer-neutral paid Parental Leave of up to 16 weeks.
