## Job Description
Design, build and improve web applications that matter.Join ACER's Software Development team as a Front‑End Developer on a 6‑month fixed‑term, full‑time contract, collaborating on digital solutions that support education and research on a global scale.This is a fixed‑term role aligned to a time‑sensitive project for a prominent Australian client, offering an exciting opportunity to contribute to innovative, high‑impact assessment solutions.About ACER The Australian Council for Educational Research (ACER) is an international, independent, not-for-profit organisation committed to improving learning through worldclass research, assessments and digital innovation. With around 400 staff across Australia, India, Malaysia, the UAE and the UK, ACER delivers high impact educational solutions to clients around the world. Our Software Development team, part of the Information Technology Division, works collaboratively across ACER to design, build and maintain high quality digital products that enable impactful research and assessment programs. We are currently seeking a passionate and experienced mid-level Front-End Developer with a solid working knowledge of JavaScript to help with a time sensitive project delivering innovative interactive assessments for a prominent Australian client. What we're looking for The primary focus of the Front-End Developer will be building interactive assessment modules based on designs provided by assessment developers. The role will provide solution design input, code development, and quality assurance. It is expected that the developer will take ownership of the quality, accessibility, security, and maintainability of the code, and be able to troubleshoot any issues that arise. The Front-End Developer will work under the Lead Software Engineer and in collaboration with the Assessment Developers, UX Designers and Software Testers. To be successful in this position, you will possess the following: Bachelor degree in Computer Science (or relevant equivalent). 3+ years commercial experience with JavaScript front-end web development. Proven track record of developing high standard, detailed, interactive web interfaces. Proven experience using OO design and development, demonstrating use of real-world design patterns and class frameworks. A high standard of engineering work and adherence to industry best practices and standards. Strong problem solving and solution design skills, and attention to detail. Keeping to project timelines at a self-managed pace. About you You are a capable and adaptable frontend developer who can hit the ground running in a fixed term role. You take pride in high-quality code, communicate clearly, and enjoy working as part of a supportive technical team. Skills and experience Essential Commercial experience in frontend or web application development Strong object-oriented design and development skills Experience with PHP and/or Java in a commercial environment Solid database design and SQL skills Experience producing technical documentation (e.g. UML, design artefacts) Understanding of software development lifecycles and methodologies Experience working across multiple platforms (Windows, macOS, Unix) Strong communication skills with the ability to engage technical and nontechnical stakeholders Excellent organisation and time management skills A proactive, "can-do" mindset and commitment to quality Qualifications Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related discipline (or equivalent experience) Why join ACER?
