## Job Description
Making a difference isn't just our purpose, it's what motivates our people every day. For us, work is not about ticking a box. It's about knowing that it matters. We make a meaningful difference every day by making it easier for our customers to focus on what matters in their lives.

As part of the MMS Group—home to trusted brands such as Maxxia, RemServ, and PSS—we help Australians make smarter, simpler decisions by delivering seamless salary packaging, novated leasing, and financial wellbeing solutions. Our digital platforms play a key role in that experience, and this role helps shape the future of how our customers interact with us online.

Here's how you will make a difference in this role…

As a Frontend Developer you will help deliver fast, accessible and modern digital experiences across our enterprise websites and customer-facing platforms. You'll play a key role in building reusable components, ensuring our web experiences meet accessibility standards, and supporting future‑focused edge‑delivery models such as AEM Edge Delivery Services.

About the role

At MMS, clear expectations help our people Be the Difference.

Your key responsibilities in this role will include:

- Developing responsive, accessible and high‑quality front-end interfaces.
- Building components using HTML5, CSS3/SASS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks such as React, Next.js or Vue.js.
- Ensuring all digital experiences meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.
- Optimising performance with a strong focus on Core Web Vitals.
- Working within Git-based workflows, including branching, code reviews and CI/CD processes.
- Contributing to reusable design standards and component governance.
- Collaborating across UX, content, SEO, backend and platform teams.
- Supporting continuous improvement initiatives and digital transformation programs.

To be considered for this role, you will possess the following:

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development or equivalent experience.
- 3+ years of professional frontend development experience in enterprise environments.
- Strong proficiency in semantic HTML5, modern CSS, and JavaScript.
- Experience with modern frameworks such as React, Next.js or Vue.js.
- Demonstrated knowledge of WCAG 2.1 AA and accessibility‑first development.
- Experience working with Git and CI/CD pipelines.

Preferred: 1–2 years' experience with AEM Sites or AEM Edge Delivery Services (EDS).

Highly desirable: Accessibility certifications such as CPACC, WAS, or similar.

Strong attention to detail, performance‑focused mindset, and effective collaboration skills.
