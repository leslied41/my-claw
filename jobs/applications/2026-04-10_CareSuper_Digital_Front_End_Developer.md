## Job Description
About us At CareSuper, we believe we're Super Together. As a profit-to-member super fund with over 600,000 members and $60 billion in assets, we're passionate about helping our members retire with confidence. We're growing fast and looking for our next superhero to join us on this exciting journey. About the role As our new Digital Front End Developer, you'll join our Member Experience and Retirement team, playing a key role in shaping our member-facing platforms. You'll champion the product roadmap by delivering intuitive, high‑quality UX and UI design solutions. If successful, a day in the life will look like this;  Own and deliver assigned initiatives end‑to‑end, from backlog prioritisation and MVP scoping through to release Contribute to the product vision, roadmap, and backlog for Member Online and mobile platforms, aligned to business goals and member insights Build, enhance, and maintain the front end of our digital products, including the member portal, mobile apps, and web‑based tools Design and prototype intuitive UX/UI solutions, continuously iterating based on user feedback, business needs, and technical considerations Act as the primary point of contact for Member Online and App initiatives and ongoing optimisations Collaborate closely with stakeholders to gather requirements and translate them into clear design and technical specifications Debug, test, and optimise solutions to ensure high standards of quality, security, and performance Maintain accurate documentation and version control across design assets and codebases  

What we're looking for  We're looking for a passionate individual who lives our values and is motivated by delivering meaningful outcomes for our members. You'll be driven by creating high‑quality digital experiences that support smarter investment decisions and positively impact our members' retirement outcomes. You'll thrive in this role if you have;  Relevant experience in design, development, and delivery of complex web or mobile applications Ability to manage initiatives through to delivery — including prioritisation, scoping, and stakeholder engagement Proficiency with Figma, including rapid prototyping and management of large-scale design systems Front-end development: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, SASS, Git, Vite Strong understanding of UX/UI design principles with a user-centric mindset Strong understanding of web and mobile standards, frontend architecture, performance optimisation, accessibility, and security Strong communication and collaboration skills — able to explain design rationale, provide actionable feedback, and produce clear documentation Analytical thinking and creative problem-solving Proactive and self-driven with a growth mindset Interest or experience in leveraging AI to improve design and development workflows or product capabilities  

Why CareSuper?  We're not just another super fund. We're a values-driven organisation that puts people first—our members and our team. Here's what you can look forward to:  Hybrid and flexible work environment 3% Superannuation Guarantee (SG) above industry minimum Matching an additional superannuation salary sacrifice of up to 2% 5 weeks of annual leave 17 weeks of parental leave for all eligible employees Holistic health and wellbeing support - free annual flu shots, skin checks, Employee Assistance Program Professional Development Rewards and Recognition Program, as well as employee discounts and cashback offers on a broad range of products and services
