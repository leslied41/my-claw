About the role
We're looking for a skilled Software Engineer to join a high-performing development team within our Technology & Operations division. This role plays a key part in delivering customer-centric digital solutions by building high-quality applications and APIs that support key strategic initiatives.
You'll work within an Agile environment, contributing across the full software development lifecycle and collaborating closely with product owners, engineers, and business stakeholders to deliver secure, scalable, and high-performance solutions.
What you'll be doing
As a Software Engineer, you will:

Design, develop, and maintain scalable, secure digital applications
Build and support APIs and microservices for digital services
Contribute actively to Agile ceremonies, sprint planning, and delivery
Implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines with automation and security embedded
Support production deployments and post-release activities

What you'll bring

Expertise in full-stack development using ReactJS, Java, Spring Boot, Gradle/Maven, Jenkins, OpenShift, and AWS
Experience supporting high-availability production systems
Experience with CI/CD techniques, container platforms, and serverless concepts
Solid knowledge of source control, build tools, API and testing frameworks
Strong understanding of Agile and Lean delivery approaches
