## Job Description
Take hold of a monster opportunity and join our Technology Engineering team as a Software Engineer - React Native. If you're a good fit for this role and live in Brisbane, we'd love you to apply.

Why join us?
You'll get 14% super as standard, to help your future come alive.
Unleash your potential and build a career to be proud of through excellent training and development opportunities.
Balance your work between in-person days at our purpose-built hubs and working from home.
In the office, enjoy end of trip facilities, a yoga and multi-faith room, parent and carer rooms, quiet rooms, BrewHub coffee facilities, and collaborative team spaces.

About the role
We're all about helping our members make the most of their money. And while they go after their goals, you can too. As a Software Engineer at Australian Retirement Trust, you will play a key role in designing, developing and supporting high‑quality application solutions that help ART remain market‑leading. Working within a collaborative technology environment, you will contribute across the full solution lifecycle and support reliable, scalable platforms for our members and internal stakeholders.

Key responsibilities include:
- Design, develop and maintain mobile applications using React Native and Expo
- Write clean, efficient, and well documented code using TypeScript and modern JavaScript frameworks.
- Analyse requirements and implement software changes in line with agreed standards, frameworks and best practices
- Build and maintain moderately complex programs, scripts and integrations, ensuring quality and performance outcomes
- Diagnose, investigate and resolve incidents and system issues, including contributing to root cause analysis
- Support configuration, testing and deployment activities across multiple environments
- Actively contribute to planning, estimation, design and feasibility discussions across platforms and technologies
- Maintain accurate and up‑to‑date documentation for systems, tools, processes and approaches
- Participate in continuous improvement initiatives, release activities and an on‑call roster as required

This role is a permanent opportunity.

Location: Brisbane.

About you
It goes without saying you'll be a great communicator with top notch interpersonal skills. We'll also expect you to pick up problems and come up with quick, creative ways to solve them. It's quite likely you tick some of the following boxes too:

- A bachelor's degree in computer science, Software Engineering or a related discipline (or equivalent experience)
- In-depth expertise and experience developing mobile applications using React Native and Expo
- Experience writing and reviewing code using TypeScript and other modern JS tools and frameworks
- Strong understanding of native mobile development (Kotlin/Java for Android and Swift/Objective-C for iOS) is an advantage
- Proven experience implementing and maintaining DevOps workflows for mobile builds and deployments
- Experience in app store management processes, including submission is an advantage
- Strong problem‑solving and analytical skills, with high attention to detail
- Experience with modern programming languages, frameworks and tools, ideally within a cloud‑based environment
- Hands‑on experience with version control, and CI/CD pipelines
- Working knowledge of software delivery methodologies such as Agile and Waterfall
- The ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non‑technical stakeholders
- Experience within financial services, highly regulated environments or superannuation will be highly regarded.
