Your New Role 
Are you an Application Engineer who enjoys building solutions end-to-end and wants to work with modern cloud, AI and automation technologies? 
 
Reporting to the Manager, Application Engineering, the Application Engineer is a hands-on technical role focused on developing, productionising and optimising business driven applications, functions and features across multiple teams and initiatives. 
 
This role suits an engineer who's seen how other corporate organisations do things, and who can work autonomously, but also enjoys collaborating with architects, product teams and other engineers. You'll work across the full lifecycle (focussing more on delivery at first, with an increasing DevOps focus to follow), helping ensure solutions are scalable, reusable and aligned to engineering best practice. We're looking for someone who is experienced with AI, someone who has used AI as a copilot in day-to-day engineering, and who has also built and delivered AI solutions (including agentic solutions) in production environments. 
 
We're looking for more than just a coder. This is a role for someone curious, adaptable and forward looking. Someone who embraces AI and automation, pushes boundaries, and is happy to challenge the status quo. Someone that will ask the questions, put forward their ideas, and is comfortable playing different roles as the Team and future ways of working evolve. 
 
What You'll Do 
 
- Design, build and enhance applications, features and functions aligned to target architecture and best practices 
- Develop and deploy solutions in a DevOps environment, embracing AI and automation first engineering approaches 
- Work closely with stakeholders, business users and other engineers to deliver high quality outcomes 
- Support operational management of applications, including monitoring, observability, incident resolution and lifecycle management 
- Identify opportunities to improve solutions through reuse, automation and new technologies 
- Contribute to engineering culture, communities of practice and shared ways of working across the Fund 
 
What You'll Need 
 
- Experience as a mid-tier application engineer (typically 3–5+ years), able to work autonomously 
- Strong experience across the Microsoft technology stack, including .NET frameworks, Azure services and Power Platform 
- Experience with programming and scripting, including Python (required), JavaScript and frontend technologies (e.g. React) 
- Exposure to DevOps, CI/CD pipelines, Agile and DevSecOps ways of working 
- Experience delivering solutions in regulated environments or financial services (highly regarded) 
- Strong problem-solving ability, collaboration skills and a proactive, learning oriented mindset 
- Interest in AI enabled and agentic solutions, and incorporating automation into engineering practices
