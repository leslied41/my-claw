## Job Description
Our Story!
DataMesh Group is revolutionising the payment systems available to merchants and retailers, delivering integrated payment capabilities and valuable customer insights through bespoke payment and data processing solutions.

The company is in an exciting growth phase, and we are looking for individuals with the appetite and energy to make impactful changes and accelerate their career development with this unique opportunity!

Smarter connections, infinite possibilities!

About the role - Build. Mentor. Impact!
We're looking for a Senior Software Engineer who's excited about tackling complex challenges, building scalable systems, and leaving a real mark in a fast-growing tech company. If you love writing clean, elegant code, mentoring others, and collaborating on products that make a difference, you'll feel right at home here.

What You'll Be Doing
- Lead technical delivery - writing efficient, scalable, and secure code.
- Design and build APIs and distributed systems that power products at scale.
- Translate business needs into smart, practical technical solutions.
- Mentor engineers, uplift team performance, and champion best practices.
- Take full ownership of features from design and prototyping through to deployment.
- Optimise performance and scalability across cloud platforms (AWS or Azure).
- Contribute to technical architecture and influence long-term engineering strategy.
- Partner with PMs, QA, designers, and fellow devs to deliver with impact.

What We're Looking For
- Strong computer science fundamentals and problem-solving ability (degree or equivalent experience).
- Proven track record building cloud-native applications (AWS/Azure).
- Proficiency with the MERN stack, RESTful APIs, and modern dev practices.
- Bonus: experience with Golang, Docker, Kubernetes.
- A growth mindset—you're curious, adaptable, and always pushing boundaries.
- A collaborative leader who thrives in a team but can also run with big projects independently.
