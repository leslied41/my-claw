## Job Description
Shape the future of mission-critical systems with intuitive design and robust engineering. Join a fast-growing, multidisciplinary engineering team building real-time, data-driven interfaces used in high-stakes environments.

About the Opportunity

We're looking for an experienced Front End Developer with specific experience working with React and TypeScript to help build the next generation of situational awareness platforms. In this role, you'll craft powerful, intuitive user interfaces that translate complex operational data into actionable intelligence — all while collaborating with a world-class team of Engineers and Data, AI and ML experts.

You'll be working in a fast-paced environment where innovation, performance, and user experience are paramount. The software you write will play a critical role in systems used across defence, security, and industrial applications.

Must Haves:
- You have AU Citizenship or AU PR

- You are available to work in the Sydney CBD office 2-3 days per week

Skills You Will Bring:
- Degree in Computer Science or a related technical discipline (or equivalent experience)
- 5+ years of commercial front-end development experience
- Strong experience with React and TypeScript
- Comfortable with complex REST APIs
- Familiar with Docker, CI/CD, Github/GitLab
- Bonus if you've worked in a startup, scale-up, or real-time systems environment
- Self-driven, collaborative, and eager to contribute to high-impact projects from day one
- Experience with AWS would also be a plus
